Explore devices

The first step in prototyping a device (or prototyping just for fun) is to select the right developer board. While Windows 10 IoT Core is designed to work on thousands of hardware configurations, below you'll find a selection of boards and developer kits we recommend to choose from when prototyping.

If specified, the devices with Full Flash Update (FFU) available will make prototyping faster since they already have a system image ready for development. If you are looking to commercialize, for optimal security, you must ship your device with a custom FFU. To learn more, refer to our IoT Core Manufacturing Guide.
