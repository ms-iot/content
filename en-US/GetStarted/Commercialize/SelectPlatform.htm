Select your platform

Microsoft works alongside Broadcom, Intel, and Qualcomm to verify support for Windows 10 IoT Core on several of the silicon vendors' System on Chips (SoCs). Picking the right platform to commercialize with is the next important step. For more information on our SoC Partners, as well as where to download Board Support Packages (BSPs) for certain boards and devices, please read our documentation on suggested boards and SoCs.
