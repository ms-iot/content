Welcome to Windows IoT at Maker Faire Bay Area

As the Windows IoT team we are constantly inspired by the enthusiasm and passion for technology represented by the Maker community. It’s for this reason that we’re so incredibly excited to be present at Maker Faire Bay Area at San Mateo to engage with you - the Maker community - directly with our technology!

If you are at the show and haven’t visited our booth then please swing by and see our live demonstrations and activitie powered by Windows 10. We have interesting projects and interactive exhibits for all ages!

You can get a taste of what you’ll see at our booth by checking out our video channel. You can build your own version of these projects through our project gallery powered by Hackster.IO.

We are located in booth 1419, Zone 10 (Maker Lab).
